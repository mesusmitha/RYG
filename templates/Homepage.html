How it works

Student


Students can get notifications about course and also they can request sponsors for financial help. Registered student can view the route map and get the clear idea how to reach the goal.

Guide


Guide can be anyone who registered, especially employed and shows the way to reach the students goal. Student can easily communicate with the guide and clarify their doubts in the process of reaching their goal.

Sponsor

Sponsor can be anyone who gets registered to help the students financially who, with the merit scores. Student can approach the sponsors in order to get any financial help if they are with the merit score in their academics.
